About the Celebration

With hearts full of gratitude, we invite you to our Valaikappu ceremony. A beautiful Tamil tradition celebrating the upcoming arrival of our little one.

This baby is truly a lucky miracle already loved by so many, and deeply cherished by us. July 11 holds a special meaning in our journey, and this new chapter feels like the most beautiful blessing yet.

We will honor tradition with Valaikappu (bangle ceremony), turmeric rituals, and divine blessings, followed by a happy get-together with music, laughter, and togetherness.

We'd also love to freeze these memories so one day our child can see the love that surrounded them even before they were born.

Your blessings will make this celebration brighter. Please come and be part of our joy and feel free to share your name suggestions too!!!
